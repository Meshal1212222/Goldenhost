Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: February 2026

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to request the deletion of your personal data from Golden Host, please follow these steps:

  1. Send a message to our WhatsApp support requesting data deletion
  2. Provide the phone number associated with your account
  3. Our team will process your request within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will delete:

  • Your contact information (name, phone number)
  • Message history and conversation records
  • Any other personal data stored in our system

Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation message once your data has been successfully deleted from our systems.
